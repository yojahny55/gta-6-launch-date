About GTA 6 Predictions

Transparency, community, and data you can trust

What Is This?

This is a community-driven tracker for GTA 6 launch date predictions. Think of it as a collective crystal ball where you can see what the gaming community really thinks—not just official dates or corporate promises.

Rockstar has announced GTA 6 will launch in November 2026, but let's be honest: after years of delays and radio silence, plenty of gamers are skeptical. This tool captures that sentiment.

Your anonymous prediction helps paint the collective sentiment

No account needed. No tracking. Just honest community data.

Why This Exists

GTA 6 has been delayed more times than we can count. Rockstar announced a release date, then pushed it back. The gaming community has every right to be skeptical.

The Problem

  • No existing tool captures community sentiment: Reddit polls disappear, Twitter threads get buried, and forum discussions are scattered
  • Gap between official dates and what fans believe: Rockstar says 2026. Fans think 2027, 2028, or "when pigs fly."
  • Community needs a voice: Gamers deserve a place to voice their predictions without corporate spin

This tracker fills that gap. It's built by a GTA fan for GTA fans, with no corporate agenda—just transparency and data.

How It Works

  1. Submit your prediction anonymously: No account required. We use a cookie to remember your prediction so you can update it later if you change your mind.
  2. We calculate a weighted median: Your prediction is included in the community median, but trolls submitting "2099" have less influence than reasonable predictions (more on this below).
  3. See how you compare: After submitting, you'll see the community consensus and how your prediction stacks up.
  4. Share results: Spread the word on Reddit, Twitter, or Discord to get more predictions and better data.

It's simple, fast, and completely anonymous. No BS, no data harvesting—just honest community sentiment.

Updates Work Across IP Changes

Your cookie is the primary identifier for your prediction. This means you can update your prediction from any IP address—whether you're switching between WiFi and mobile data, using a VPN, or accessing from a different location.

  • Mobile users: Update from WiFi, LTE, or 5G—your cookie stays valid
  • VPN users: Your prediction follows you, not your IP address
  • Network changes: Switching networks? No problem. Your cookie is your identity.

Lost Your Cookie?

If you cleared your browser cookies, you'll need to submit a new prediction. Each IP address can only submit once, but your cookie allows updates from any IP.

The Algorithm (Transparency)

We know what you're thinking: "Is this rigged? Can trolls skew the results?"

The answer is no, and here's why. We use a weighted median algorithm that gives different predictions different levels of influence based on how reasonable they are.

How Weighting Works

Prediction Range Weight Explanation
Within 5 years of official date (2025-2030) 1.0 (Full weight) Reasonable predictions get full influence
5-50 years out (2030-2075) 0.3 (Reduced weight) Far-out predictions get less influence
Beyond 50 years (2075+) 0.1 (Minimal weight) Troll predictions barely count

Why This Matters

Someone submitting "2099" as a joke gets only 10% of the influence of someone predicting 2027. This keeps trolls from ruining the data while still being democratic.

Plain English: Reasonable predictions count more. Troll predictions barely count. The median stays meaningful.

Privacy & Data

We take privacy seriously. Here's exactly what we collect and why:

What We Collect

  • Your prediction date: Obviously, we need this to calculate the median
  • Cookie ID: A unique identifier stored in your browser to track your prediction (so you can update it later)
  • Hashed IP address: Your IP is encrypted (hashed) before storage to prevent spam. We never store your actual IP address in plain text.

What We DON'T Collect

  • No email addresses
  • No names or personal info
  • No tracking across other websites
  • No selling your data to third parties (ever)

Cookie Usage

We use cookies only to remember your prediction. No tracking, no analytics cookies unless you explicitly consent. You can manage cookie preferences in the banner at the bottom of the page.

Data Retention (How Long We Keep Your Data)

We practice data minimization and automatically delete old data according to these retention policies:

  • Predictions: Kept indefinitely (core product value—you expect your prediction to persist)
  • Analytics data: Automatically deleted after 24 months (Cloudflare handles this)
  • Server logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days (daily cleanup at 2 AM UTC)
  • Rate limiting & cache data: Automatically expires (60 seconds for rate limits, 5 minutes for cache)

Our automated cleanup system runs daily to ensure we only keep what's necessary. This minimizes data storage while maintaining service quality—exactly what GDPR requires.

Your Data, Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Update your prediction: Just submit a new date—we'll update your existing prediction
  • Delete your data: Visit our data deletion form to request deletion (GDPR "Right to be Forgotten")
  • View your data: Your prediction is displayed when you visit the homepage

For full details, check out our Privacy Policy.
